¿Por qué elegirnos?

Tecnologia de Vanguardia

Utilizamos los últimos avances en odontología digital para ofrecerte diagnósticos precisos y tratamientos eficaces. Desde escaneos 3D hasta impresiones digitales, nuestras herramientas nos permiten planificar y ejecutar procedimientos con máxima precisión y comodidad.

Experiencia y Profesionalismo

Nuestro equipo está formado por odontólogos altamente capacitados y con amplia experiencia en diversas especialidades. Estamos aquí para garantizar que recibas el mejor cuidado en cada visita.

Atención Personalizada

Cada paciente es único y merece un tratamiento que se ajuste a sus necesidades específicas. En Oasis Smiles, diseñamos planes de tratamiento personalizados para asegurar que obtengas los mejores resultados.

Nuestros Servicios de Primera Clase

Full Mouth Dental Implants

If you have a lot of missing or failing teeth, you may want to consider full arch dental implants. These permanent teeth replacements are surgically implanted to four or more posts in your jawbone, completely restoring your smile and nearly all of your biting and chewing power. By creating chrome guides for dental implant surgery, Dr. Barriere can provide faster, more efficient surgery and quicker recovery times.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Whether you need crowns, veneers, Lumineers® or teeth whitening, West LA Implants is proud to provide many forms of cosmetic dentistry. Each treatment is custom crafted to your specific needs, so your smile makeover will be as unique as you are!

Wisdom Teeth Extraction

Are your wisdom teeth becoming impacted? Have they just started to grow in, or have they been bothering you for years? No matter your situation, you deserve to live your life free of pain, and Dr. Barriere has more than a decade of experience removing wisdom teeth in Los Angeles.

Sedation Dentistry

Almost every patient we see experiences some degree of dental anxiety. We offer sedation services to our patients help them combat the fear that often is associated with a trip the dentist and to help them get the care they need.
